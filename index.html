About Little's Preschool

Little's Preschool is committed to providing a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can grow, learn, and thrive. Our dedicated team of experienced educators is passionate about fostering each child's unique potential.

Located in beautiful Hood River, Oregon, we combine evidence-based early childhood practices with play-based learning to support the developmental milestones of infants through preschoolers.

We believe that quality childcare is a partnership between our educators and your family. We're committed to open communication, transparency, and creating an environment where every child feels valued and supported.
